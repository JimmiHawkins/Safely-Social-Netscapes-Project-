Teenagers

Potential dangers and what you can do to prevent them

If you ever feel uncomfortable or confused about a situation, don’t be scared about going to an adult to ask about the dangers of being online if you feel like you are in danger and there is no solution. You aren’t expected to know all of the dangers when it comes to your safety online, which is why it’s always good to approach an adult if you feel like you are unsure about something.

Potential Dangers

Cyberbullying

When someone is sending you mean messages over and over again purposfully trying to make you upset. This can be in the form of messages, posts, images, etc.

Prevention

If this person makes you feel uncomfortable you should block them on the social media you are being messaged on. This should prevent them from contacting you again. If you do not know how to do this, ask your parent.

Grooming

It's important to recognise when you are being groomed. This is similar to cyberbullying except the groomer will send you messages to gradually gain your friendship to then later use it against you by getting you to take part in bad things

Prevention

The first step is to recognise if this persoon has bad intentions. Do you know this person? Are they being overly freindly for no reason? Are they trying to convince you to do things you do not want to? If you said no to all of these then they are most likely bad news so blocking them is your best bet. To prevnt this from happening again, do not accept friend requests from people you do not know online.

OverSharing

This is when you display or tell people inforamtion about yourself, where you live, where you like to go, etc.

Prevention

Do not tell people any personal information like phone numbers, adresses, family names, your name, where you go to school, or anything like that. People can use this inforamation to cause you harm.

Sexting

This is when you send sexual texts, videos, or images of yourself for people to see over the internet. You should never do this even if you know the person on the other end, as it is illegal.

Prevention

Do not send anyone send sexual texts, videos, or images of yourself. If someone is asking for any of these block them immediatly.
