How to set-up a safe profile online

Location

Make sure your location is not on your profile or set to public.

Privacy

Set your discoverablity settings on your account to private to prevent people you do not know being able to see your profile

Profile Picture

Make sure your profile photo does not show any personal information or contain an image so revealing that people can work out your location

User Name

Do not use your full name as your username as people may use this information to find out more about you. The only excption would be if your account is set to private and you only add your real-life friends as contacts

Bio

Make sure you don't use any private information your bio like phone number, location, where you go to school, etc.

Check Everything

Go through each step and make sure your profile is safe before creating it
